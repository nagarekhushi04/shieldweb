# ShieldWeb3 User Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the ShieldWeb3 ecosystem! This guide will walk you through the entire process of setting up your wallet, testing the system, and participating in the decentralized security network.

## Step 1: Install Freighter Wallet
ShieldWeb3 relies on the Stellar ecosystem for identity and threat verification.
1. Visit [freighter.app](https://freighter.app) and install the browser extension.
2. Follow the setup instructions to create a new wallet and secure your recovery phrase.

## Step 2: Switch to Stellar Testnet
1. Open the Freighter extension.
2. Click the gear icon (Settings) in the top right.
3. Go to **Network** and select **Testnet**.

## Step 3: Copy Your Public Key
On the main screen of the Freighter extension, click on your wallet address (it begins with a `G`). This will copy your public key to your clipboard.

## Step 4: Fund Your Testnet Wallet
To interact with our smart contracts, you need Testnet XLM to pay for transaction fees.
1. Navigate to the [Stellar Laboratory Account Creator](https://laboratory.stellar.org/account-creator).
2. Paste your public key.
3. Click "Fund Account".

## Step 5: Verify Your Balance
1. Visit [Stellar Expert Testnet Explorer](https://stellar.expert/explorer/testnet/account/[YOUR_KEY]). (Replace `[YOUR_KEY]` with your actual public key).
2. Verify that your balance shows 10,000 XLM.

## Step 6: Connect to ShieldWeb3
1. Visit [shieldweb3.vercel.app](https://shieldweb3.vercel.app).
2. Click the "Connect Wallet" button in the top right corner.
3. Approve the connection request in the Freighter popup.

## Step 7: Test the Threat Checker
On the home page, locate the threat checker input and try these 3 example URLs to test the real-time AI and blockchain verification:
- **Safe:** `https://stellar.org`
- **Malicious:** `http://example-fake-wallet-phishing.com` (Example)
- **Suspicious:** `http://unverified-crypto-airdrop.xyz` (Example)

## Step 8: Submit a Threat Report
Help protect the community!
1. Navigate to the **Report** tab.
2. Enter a designated malicious URL (e.g., `http://scam-site-demo.com`).
3. Select a threat category and severity level.
4. Click **Submit** and sign the transaction using Freighter to register the threat on the Stellar blockchain.

## Step 9: Check Your Rewards
By contributing to the network, you earn SHW3 tokens!
1. Navigate to your **Dashboard**.
2. Verify that your submitted report is listed as "Verified" (this may take a short time to process).
3. Check your SHW3 Token Balance reflecting the reward for your valid report.
